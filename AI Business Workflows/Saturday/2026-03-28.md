# Saturday — HR/Recruiting AI Business Workflows

**Date pulled:** 2026-03-28 (Asia/Dhaka)  
**Window targeted:** last 30 days  
**Theme:** How businesses are using AI to replace/automate HR and recruiting functions

## Executive summary
The HR landscape is rapidly evolving with the introduction of agentic AI, which goes beyond traditional automation to enable proactive decision-making in HR functions. This report outlines practical use cases for businesses looking to implement AI in HR and recruiting, emphasizing the importance of human oversight in high-stakes scenarios. The focus is on actionable workflows that can be integrated with existing systems to enhance efficiency and decision-making.

## Top 5 practical use cases
1. **Automated Candidate Screening**: AI agents can analyze resumes against job descriptions, significantly reducing the time HR spends on initial candidate filtering.
2. **Interview Scheduling**: Automating the coordination of interviews can free up HR personnel to focus on strategic tasks.
3. **Onboarding Automation**: Streamlining the onboarding process through AI can ensure new hires have a consistent experience while reducing administrative burdens.
4. **Employee Self-Service Portals**: AI can manage routine inquiries, allowing HR to focus on complex issues that require human intervention.
5. **Predictive Analytics for Retention**: AI tools can analyze employee data to predict turnover risks and suggest retention strategies.

## Best first use case to implement
**Automated Candidate Screening** is the best first use case. It provides immediate ROI by reducing the time spent on manual resume reviews. Implementing this workflow can be done with existing ATS systems and requires minimal upfront investment. 

### Implementation Steps:
- Integrate AI tools with the current ATS.
- Define clear criteria for candidate evaluation.
- Set up a human-in-the-loop system for final candidate approval to mitigate risks.

## OpenClaw workflow mapping
1. **Input**: Job description and candidate resumes.
2. **Process**:
   - AI analyzes resumes based on defined criteria.
   - Generates a shortlist of candidates.
   - Sends notifications to HR for review.
3. **Output**: Shortlisted candidates ready for interviews.

## 7-day KPI pilot
### Objectives:
- Reduce time-to-hire by 30%.
- Increase the quality of shortlisted candidates as measured by interview success rates.

### Day-by-Day Plan:
- **Day 1**: Set up AI screening criteria and integrate with ATS.
- **Day 2**: Run a test batch of resumes against the AI system.
- **Day 3**: Review AI-generated shortlists with HR team.
- **Day 4**: Conduct interviews with shortlisted candidates.
- **Day 5**: Collect feedback on candidate quality from interviewers.
- **Day 6**: Analyze time saved in the screening process.
- **Day 7**: Review pilot results and adjust AI parameters as needed.
