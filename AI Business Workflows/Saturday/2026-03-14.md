# Saturday — HR/Recruiting AI Business Workflows

**Date pulled:** 2026-03-14 (Asia/Dhaka)  
**Window targeted:** last 30 days  
**Theme:** How businesses are using AI to replace/automate HR and recruiting functions  

## Executive summary
The landscape of HR and recruiting is rapidly evolving with AI technologies automating traditional processes. Businesses are increasingly adopting AI-driven tools to enhance efficiency, reduce manual workloads, and improve candidate experiences. However, while automation offers significant advantages, it is essential to approach these technologies with a critical eye, ensuring that human oversight remains integral to decision-making processes.

## Top 5 practical use cases
1. **Automated Resume Screening**: Tools like MokaHR and hireEZ utilize AI to screen resumes, significantly reducing the time spent on initial candidate evaluations. This allows HR teams to focus on higher-value tasks.
   
2. **Interview Scheduling Automation**: Platforms such as Lever and Nuroblox automate the scheduling of interviews, minimizing back-and-forth communication and ensuring a smoother candidate experience.

3. **Candidate Communication**: AI-driven chatbots can handle routine inquiries from candidates, providing timely responses and freeing HR personnel for more complex interactions.

4. **Data-Driven Candidate Insights**: AI tools analyze candidate data to predict success rates and cultural fit, enabling more informed hiring decisions.

5. **Onboarding Automation**: AI can streamline the onboarding process by automating document collection and training schedules, ensuring new hires have a seamless transition into the company.

## Best first use case to implement
**Automated Resume Screening** is the best first use case for businesses looking to pilot AI in HR. This implementation can quickly demonstrate value by reducing the time spent on initial candidate evaluations while maintaining a human-in-the-loop approach for final decisions. By using tools like MokaHR, companies can achieve up to 3× faster candidate screening with high accuracy rates.

## OpenClaw workflow mapping
1. **Input**: Job descriptions and candidate resumes.
2. **Process**: 
   - AI scans resumes against job requirements.
   - Human reviewers validate top candidates.
   - Automated notifications sent to candidates about their application status.
3. **Output**: Shortlisted candidates ready for interviews.

## 7-day KPI pilot
- **Day 1-2**: Set up the AI screening tool and integrate it with existing ATS.
- **Day 3**: Run a batch of resumes through the system.
- **Day 4**: Collect data on screening speed and accuracy.
- **Day 5**: Review shortlisted candidates with HR team.
- **Day 6**: Gather feedback from HR on the process and candidate quality.
- **Day 7**: Analyze results and prepare a report on efficiency gains and areas for improvement.
