# Saturday — HR/Recruiting AI Business Workflows

**Date pulled:** 2026-04-25 (Asia/Dhaka)  
**Window targeted:** last 30 days  
**Theme:** How businesses are using AI to replace/automate HR and recruiting functions  

## Executive summary
In the past month, businesses have increasingly adopted agentic AI to streamline HR and recruiting processes. This technology automates end-to-end workflows, from candidate sourcing to interview scheduling, significantly reducing manual effort and improving efficiency. However, organizations must remain cautious, ensuring human oversight where necessary to mitigate risks associated with bias and compliance.

## Top 5 practical use cases
1. **Candidate Sourcing**: Agentic AI autonomously searches multiple job boards and social media platforms, refining criteria based on previous hiring successes.
2. **Screening and Shortlisting**: AI evaluates candidates against a holistic profile, identifying non-obvious matches and reducing screening time by up to 75%.
3. **Interview Scheduling**: Automated agents coordinate interview times across stakeholders, minimizing back-and-forth communication.
4. **Feedback Collection**: AI gathers candidate feedback post-interview, providing insights for continuous improvement in the hiring process.
5. **Onboarding Coordination**: Post-hire, AI manages onboarding tasks, ensuring new hires have the necessary resources and information to start effectively.

## Best first use case to implement
**Automated Screening and Shortlisting**: This use case is ideal for a pilot program due to its clear parameters and measurable outcomes. By implementing an agentic AI solution to handle initial candidate screenings, businesses can significantly reduce the time spent on this task while maintaining quality through consistent evaluation criteria. 

## OpenClaw workflow mapping
1. **Input**: Job requisition details and candidate profiles.
2. **Process**:
   - AI sources candidates from multiple platforms.
   - AI screens candidates based on predefined criteria.
   - AI generates a shortlist and provides rationale for selections.
3. **Output**: A ranked list of candidates ready for human review and interview scheduling.

## 7-day KPI pilot
- **Day 1**: Set up the AI system with job requisition details and screening criteria.
- **Days 2-3**: Run the AI screening process on a batch of candidates.
- **Days 4-5**: Review AI-generated shortlist and compare against human evaluations.
- **Days 6-7**: Measure KPIs:
  - Time taken for screening (target: reduce from 8 hours to 90 minutes).
  - Quality of shortlisted candidates (feedback from hiring managers).
  - Candidate engagement rates (response rates to outreach).
