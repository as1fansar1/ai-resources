## Executive summary
The integration of agentic AI into accounting workflows is transforming how firms manage their operations. By automating routine tasks, these systems enable accountants to focus on higher-value activities such as analysis and strategic decision-making. This report outlines practical use cases for implementing AI in accounting, emphasizing workflows that can be realistically executed with tools like OpenClaw.

## Top 5 practical use cases
1. **Automated Invoice Processing**: AI systems can capture, interpret, and categorize invoices, significantly reducing manual entry errors and processing time.
2. **Expense Management**: Automate the tracking and approval of expenses, allowing for real-time visibility and compliance checks.
3. **Financial Reporting**: Generate draft financial reports automatically, flagging discrepancies and ensuring data accuracy before human review.
4. **Tax Preparation**: AI can assist in preparing tax documents by pulling relevant data and suggesting deductions, streamlining the compliance process.
5. **Client Communication Automation**: Use AI to manage routine client inquiries and follow-ups, freeing up accountants for more complex interactions.

## Best first use case to implement
**Automated Invoice Processing** is the best first use case. This workflow addresses a high-volume, high-error area in accounting. By implementing an AI system that captures and categorizes invoices, firms can achieve quick wins in efficiency and accuracy. Human oversight is still necessary for final approvals, ensuring compliance and accuracy.

## OpenClaw workflow mapping
1. **Data Capture**: Integrate OpenClaw with document management systems to automatically pull invoices.
2. **Data Extraction**: Use AI to extract key data points (e.g., amounts, dates, vendor information).
3. **Categorization**: Automatically categorize expenses based on historical data and predefined rules.
4. **Approval Workflow**: Route invoices for approval based on thresholds (e.g., amounts over a certain limit).
5. **Integration with Accounting Systems**: Ensure that processed invoices flow directly into the accounting ledger, minimizing manual entry.

## 7-day KPI pilot
- **Day 1**: Set up OpenClaw and integrate with existing systems.
- **Day 2-3**: Train the AI on historical invoice data to improve accuracy.
- **Day 4**: Begin processing a batch of invoices through the system.
- **Day 5**: Monitor and analyze the accuracy of data extraction and categorization.
- **Day 6**: Gather feedback from the accounting team on the workflow.
- **Day 7**: Assess time saved and error reduction compared to manual processing.
