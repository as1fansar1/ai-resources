# Monday — Marketing AI Business Workflows
**Date pulled:** 2026-02-23 (UTC)  
**Window targeted:** last 30 days  
**Theme:** practical AI automation/replacement workflows for marketing teams

## Executive summary
Last-30-day signal quality indicates a clear pattern: teams are moving from one-off generative tasks to **bounded agentic automation** in campaign ops (budget shifts, creative iteration, lead routing, and reporting), while keeping humans in approval roles for spend, brand, and risk.

Practical near-term value is strongest in workflows where:
1) inputs are structured (ad + CRM + analytics),  
2) objective functions are explicit (CPA, pipeline, conversion), and  
3) decisions can be constrained by guardrails.

## Top 5 practical use cases
1. **Campaign budget reallocation copilot (daily)**
   - Auto-detect under/over-performing segments and propose budget moves by channel/campaign.
   - Human approves spend shifts above threshold.

2. **Creative variant production + QA workflow**
   - Generate ad variants (copy/image/video prompts), run policy/brand checks, queue top candidates for review.

3. **Lead-intent scoring + routing automation**
   - Score leads from behavioral + CRM signals and route high-intent accounts to sales sequences automatically.

4. **Always-on lifecycle personalization**
   - Trigger dynamic email/web/message variants by funnel stage and observed behavior.

5. **Weekly marketing insights brief (exec-ready)**
   - Pull cross-channel performance, surface anomalies, explain likely causes, and recommend actions.

## Best first use case to implement
**Campaign budget reallocation copilot (daily, human-in-the-loop).**

Why first:
- Fast measurable ROI in 1 week (CPA/CAC and wasted spend are visible quickly).
- Lower organizational friction vs. full autonomous campaign management.
- Uses existing data sources most teams already have (ads + analytics + CRM).

## OpenClaw workflow mapping
1. **Trigger**
   - Cron at fixed daily time (e.g., 07:00 UTC).
2. **Ingest**
   - Pull last 24h performance snapshots from ad platforms + analytics + CRM exports.
3. **Evaluate**
   - Score entities against guardrails (min volume, max CPA drift, spend caps).
4. **Decide**
   - Generate ranked reallocation proposals with confidence scores and risk notes.
5. **Approve**
   - Route proposed actions to a human approval step for high-impact changes.
6. **Execute**
   - Apply approved updates; log before/after state.
7. **Report**
   - Publish concise daily summary and weekly trend brief.

## 7-day KPI pilot
- **Primary KPI:** blended CPA change vs previous 7-day baseline.
- **Secondary KPIs:**
  - CTR lift on AI-generated/selected variants
  - Conversion rate lift on prioritized segments
  - % spend reallocated from low-efficiency to high-efficiency entities
  - Human review time saved (minutes/day)
- **Guardrail KPIs:**
  - Brand/policy violation rate
  - Overspend incidents (count)
  - % recommendations overridden by humans
