## Cadence

Recommended cadence for this research: **twice per week, Tuesday and Friday mornings**.

Why Tuesday and Friday:

- **Tuesday** captures Monday/early-week product launches, blog posts, GitHub trend shifts, and newsletter coverage.
- **Friday** captures the full week’s launches and practitioner workflows before weekend noise.
- The spacing avoids redundant daily reports while keeping trend discovery fresh.
